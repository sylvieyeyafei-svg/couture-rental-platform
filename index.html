03 / PROJECT RATIONALE

PLATFORM THESIS · 2026

A missing connection
between insight and access.

Luxury trend intelligence influences the industry long before it reaches the individual. This project asks: what if that intelligence could guide a more personal, circular way to experience couture?

01

THE PROBLEM

Trend knowledge stops at the report.

Runway analysis and forecasting shape production, yet rarely help consumers make more informed wardrobe decisions. The result is a gap between what the industry knows and what people can meaningfully access.

02

THE COMPETITOR GAP

Rental platforms offer inventory, not interpretation.

Research across Rent the Runway, HURR, Une Robe Un Soir, and WGSN revealed a structural disconnect: rental provides access, while forecasting provides insight. Few services unite the two.

03

THE PROPOSED SOLUTION

Consultation-driven rental.

Runway to Wardrobe translates runway signals and brand analysis into personalized, rentable couture styling. A stylist becomes the bridge between cultural insight, individual expression, and circular luxury.

Runway Signals→Brand Analysis→Personal Consultation→Rentable Couture Look
